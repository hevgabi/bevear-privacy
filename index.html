2. Description of Service

Bevear is a personal finance tracking application that allows you to:

  • Record and categorize income, expenses, and transfers
  • Manage multiple wallets and accounts (cash, bank, e-wallet, and credit card)
  • Set and track daily, weekly, and monthly budgets
  • Create and track savings goals
  • Maintain a wishlist of planned purchases
  • Track pay-later, installment, and buy-now-pay-later obligations
  • Track money owed to or by you (debts)
  • Track investment holdings for personal reference (portfolio)
  • Generate spending and income reports, with PDF and CSV export
  • Back up data to your own Google Drive account

Bevear does not move, hold, or have access to your actual money — it only stores the records and figures you choose to enter or import.

Investment, debt, and price figures you enter (e.g., in Portfolio) are for personal reference only and are not sourced, verified, or updated by us, unless you manually enter or update them.
