Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bevear ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is stored, and what rights you have in relation to it.

By using the Bevear mobile application ("App"), you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and username. If you sign in with Google, we receive your basic Google profile information (name, email address, and profile photo, if available) through Google Sign-In.

Financial Data You Enter

Bevear is built to store financial information locally on your device first, using an encrypted local database. The financial data you manually enter or import includes:

  • Transactions (income, expenses, transfers), including amounts, dates, categories, tags, notes, and optional receipt images
  • Wallet information (balances, wallet type, and provider, such as a specific bank or e-wallet)
  • Budgets (weekly, monthly, and daily plans, including fixed expenses and envelopes)
  • Savings goals and progress
  • Wishlist items, including any product links or images you add
  • Pay-later and installment plans
  • Debts owed to or by you, including the names of people associated with those debts that you choose to enter
  • Portfolio entries (investment asset names, tickers, quantities, and prices you manually enter)

If you sign in or enable cloud sync, this data is also synced to and stored in our Firebase Firestore backend, scoped to your individual account.

Authentication Data

We use Firebase Authentication to manage sign-up and login. If you sign in with Google, we receive only the basic profile information described above — we do not receive your Google account password.

Device and Diagnostic Information

We may collect basic device information, such as device type, operating system version, and app version, for crash reporting and to help us maintain and improve the App.

Backup Data

If you choose to use the Google Drive backup feature, your financial data is exported and stored as a file in a Bevear-specific folder within your own personal Google Drive account. We do not store a copy of this backup on our servers, and we do not access or read any other files in your Google Drive.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or require access to your actual bank account credentials, card numbers, or payment account passwords. The wallet and provider information you enter (such as "GCash" or "BDO") is a label you choose for organizational purposes — it is not a live connection to your real bank or e-wallet account, and the App cannot access, move, or view real funds in those accounts.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App's features
  • Create, secure, and manage your account
  • Store and sync your financial data across your devices, if you choose to sign in
  • Generate the reports, summaries, and notifications you request within the App
  • Process feedback and support requests you send us
  • Send important service-related notifications (e.g., budget alerts, bill reminders, goal milestones) — these are generated and delivered locally on your device based on your own data
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Diagnose and fix crashes or technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

AI-Assisted Features

If you use AI-assisted features such as receipt scanning or smart quick-entry, the image or text you provide for that specific transaction is sent to a third-party AI service (via our backend proxy) solely to extract structured data (such as item names, amounts, and categories) and return it to the App. This content is used only to generate that suggested entry and is not stored by us beyond what is necessary to process the request and apply reasonable rate-limiting and abuse prevention.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using a combination of methods:

  • Local storage: Your primary data lives in an encrypted local database on your device.
  • Cloud storage (if signed in): Synced data is stored using Google Firebase services, which employ industry-standard encryption and security measures. Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL; data at rest is encrypted on Firebase's servers; each user's cloud data is isolated by account and protected by Firebase security rules requiring proper authentication.
  • Your own Google Drive (if backup is enabled): Backup files are stored in your personal Drive account, governed by your own Google account security.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to enable the App's biometric lock feature for added protection on your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information or financial data to third parties. Your financial data is yours.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your consent — when you explicitly direct us to (for example, exporting and sharing a CSV or PDF report yourself)
  • To comply with the law — when required by valid legal process, court order, or government request
  • To protect rights and safety — to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users or the public
  • With service providers — limited service providers who help us operate the App's backend (such as Google Firebase/Cloud, and our AI processing proxy for receipt-scanning features), who only process data as needed to provide their service to us
  • Business transfers — if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Policy or a policy offering equivalent protections

6. Google Drive Backup

If you use the backup feature, the App requests limited access to an app-specific folder in your Google Drive. We:

  • Access and write only to the Bevear-specific data folder
  • Do not read, modify, or delete any other files in your Drive
  • Do not store a copy of your backup data on our own servers
  • Allow you to revoke this access at any time through your Google Account security settings, which will stop future backups but will not delete files already saved in your Drive

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including the right to:

  • Access — view all data stored in your account through the App at any time
  • Export — export your transaction data as a CSV file, or generate PDF reports, directly from the App
  • Correct — update your profile information and any financial entry at any time through the App
  • Delete — delete individual entries, or your entire account and all associated data, at any time through the Settings screen
  • Withdraw consent — stop using optional features (such as Google Sign-In, cloud sync, or Drive backup) at any time; the App will continue to function in local/guest mode

When you delete your account, all data associated with that account is permanently removed from our servers, generally within 30 days. Data stored only locally on your device is removed when you uninstall the App or clear its local storage.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under applicable laws (such as the GDPR or the CCPA), including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 11.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account and synced financial data for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to provide the App's features to you. If you delete your account, we delete associated server-side data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain information for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes for a longer period. Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us so we can take appropriate action, including deleting that information.

10. International Data Transfers

Our backend service providers (such as Google Firebase) may process and store data in countries other than your own. By using the App, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries that may have data protection laws different from those in your country of residence. We take steps to ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection requirements.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, features, or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
